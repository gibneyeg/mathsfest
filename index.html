Welcome to our MathsFest 2022 page.Here we will give you information about MathsFest 2022 set to take place next year.
This year MathsFest is set to be better than ever with talks from various teachers and lecturers
from around the country taking place across 2 days in 2022.We hope that you will take your time to fully go through our website.
Where we have a quiz game for you to play.



Information


MathsFest 2022 will take place in the Convention Center in dublin from the 23rd of March to the 25th.
It will cost 5 euro for entry but all the money earned will be given to to a charity voted by the people who attend.
There will be talks every hour each day from nine am to three pm.The topics of these talks will depend on who is speaking.

Prize


Another thing we want to inform you about the event is that there will also be a prize.When you buy a ticket you will automatically be put in a draw
There are three items to be won; a 100 euro amazon giftcard,a MathsFest tshirt and lastly a mathsmatics mug
